About Us

Study GRC is a non-profit organization committed to building a more secure future by fostering community, education, and free, accessible resources in Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC).

Founded in February 2024, evolving from an informal study group, we're all about making the world safer by sharing knowledge about Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC).

Our Mission

Empower individuals at all stages of their GRC journey by creating an inclusive, supportive environment.

Community Driven

Built by the community, for the community. Free resources and collaborative learning for everyone.

Free & Accessible

Standing against paywalls and barriers. We drive for free to low-cost materials that are easily accessible to all.
